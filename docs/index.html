Storyteller

Troy Augustus Carpenter

This website is home to free and unlicensed storytelling work of Troy Augustus Carpenter. I make both original stories and fan-fiction of existing stories. Media formats are currently only text based literature. Future formats include webcomics. All stories are available as downloadable PDFs for free. You can view, print, and share them as you please.

Original Universes

Updraftia

Updraftia is a fantasy saga with wizards, knights, and fantastical creatures. It is slated for ten main volumes.

Fan Fictions

Ninjago: Unplugged

Ninjago: Unplugged is a LEGO Ninjago fan-fiction. It takes place when the Ninja are adults and is more gritty and mature than the animated series as it is aimed at teens rather than children.

Dragons: Workers of Berk

Dragons: Workers of Berk is a How to Train Your Dragon fan-fiction. It takes place in the two years between Defenders of Berk and Race to the Edge.

Kung Fu Panda: The Furious Five and the Four Constellations

Kung Fu Panda: The Furious Five and the Four Constellations is a Kung Fu Panda Fan-Fiction. As the name suggests, it is a team-up between the Furious Five and the Four Constellations.

NEXO Knights: Resurrected

NEXO Knights: Resurrected is a LEGO: NEXO Knights fan-fiction. It takes place after the events of the TV series and includes the main ensemble of characters.

Ninjago: Art of the Ninja

Ninjago: Art of the Ninja is a discontinued fan-fiction based on LEGO Ninjago. It ran from early 2017 to mid 2018.
