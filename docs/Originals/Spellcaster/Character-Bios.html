Spellcaster Characters

Pandora

Pandora is the main character in Spellcaster. He blames himself for problems in the world. He is often depressed and sometimes angry and excited. He makes many mistakes. He is 16 and he is Spellcasting prodigy.

Ethoxi

Ethoxi's name is a deviant of the spelling of "ethos." She is wise, noble, and has a strong moral compass. This 15 year old does not hesitate to speak draconically.

Pasha

Pasha, 16, is the son of Lion Kingdom King Ilvia. Pasha is whimiscal and bad at tasks that demand responsibility. He is kind to people but often fails at his more sophisticated duties. He shies away from tasks of responsibility and prefers creative and inventive pursuits. He doesn't do well viewing reports or with his education. He shines his brightest in interacting with subjects, to where he does a good job opening up his heart to their problems.

Myla

Myla is 15 years old and a capable knight of the Lions. She is driven and highly competitive. If there is a competition to be had, Myla is the most likely to enlist. She is a soldier type and lives hardcore and without frills.

Ilvia

Ilvia is the King of the Lions and father to Prince Pasha. Ilvia is stern and particular; it is his way or the highway. He is agressive but also humble and protective.

Ahtuhimaway

Ahtuhimaway is the Dark Lord in Spellcaster. He is evil but speaks kindly and with a sense of morality. He is the puppeteer over many powerful people in Magicus.

Wackers

Wacktholomew, or as he prefers, Wackers, serves as the comedic relief. He is eccentric and often dense. This 15 year old is protective of other's feelings and often assumes the role of comedian to protect them.

Excel

Excellent, or Excel, doesn't excel at many things. This 13 year old is often obtuse and uncool.
