World of Naturu

Centuries ago the Islands of Naturu were unpopulated with humans. Then, humans shipwrecked on the New Zealand-sized hidden island chain. They each, in time, forgave their society given names and became named after animals. Examples of these names include Gray Wolf, Bald Eagle, White-Tailed Deer, etc. Naturu granted them magical abilities over nature. They formed into Seven Tribes based on which aspect of nature they had magic over. Each tribe has a primal desire which they represent.

The Seven Magics

Ground

Desire: Survival

Description: Continuation of ones life in some form, no matter how unpleasant.

Power Over: Ground and inorganic things that come from the ground. This includes stone, metal, precious metals, and gems.

Tribal Location: Goliath Cave

Ocean

Desire: Ease

Description: Go with the flow, expend as little willpower as possible.

Power Over: Waterways and unfrozen water.

Tribal Location: Surf Island

Technology

Desire: Comfort

Description: Avoidance of bodily pain, hunger, and thirst.

Power Over: Manmade tools, structures, and buildings.

Tribal Location: River Town

Life

Desire: Lust

Description: Sexual pleasure no matter how costly.

Power Over: Plants and animals.

Tribal Location: Thick Forest

Sun

Desire: Status

Description: One's reputation and knowledge in the minds of others.

Power Over: Sunlight and fire.

Tribal Location: Sunforce Peak

Ice

Desire: Guilt

Description: One's children being protected and norished.

Power Over: Frozen water. This includes snow, ice, hail, glaciers, icerbergs, etc.

Tribal Location: Northern Tundra

Sky

Desire: Joy

Description: Fun and laughter alongside others. Sky Mages love the fun of people which makes them monklike as they are afraid to cause grief.

Power Over: Wind and storms.

Tribal Location: Stormy Peak

The Tribes Good/ Evil or Both?

In "Legends of Naturu" each tribe has a turn to be both heroic and villainous in their many conflicts.
