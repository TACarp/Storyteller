Updraftia Series

Updraftia is an online and free fantasy novel series broy Troy Augustus Carpenter.

"Volumes" versus "Issues"?

"Volumes" are the main serialized storyline. "Issues" are monthly shorter single-edition stories that focus on minor characters and plot-lines. It is suggested to start with the volumes and read the issues for extra spin-off content.
