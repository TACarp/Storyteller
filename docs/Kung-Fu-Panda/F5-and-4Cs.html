Kung Fu Panda: The Furious Five and the Four Constellations

Kung Fu Panda: The Furious Five and the Four Constellations is a Kung Fu Panda fan-fiction. As the name suggests, it is a team-up between the the Furious Five and the Four Constellations. The premise is Po decides the Four Constellations need a more diverse Kung Fu instruction than just his so he takes them to the Jade Palace to be instructed by Shifu and the Furious Five. As ever, some characters are planning to do bad things and it is Po and his allie's job to stop them. It will be an untold number of chapters long. It is written by Troy Augustus Carpenter
